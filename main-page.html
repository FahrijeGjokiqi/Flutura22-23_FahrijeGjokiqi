The 80 Greatest Sneakers of the ’80s

The Ben Affleck-directed "Air" chronicles the game-changing (sorry) partnership between Nike and an up-and-coming basketball player named Michael Jordan, but the then-underdog athletic brand and its now-ubiquitous swoop is actually minimally present among the costumes. "It's not a Nike ad," says costume designer Charlese Antoinette. "[The costumes] just needed to really sell that we were in the '80s. This is the time before Nike became what we know now — before the Jordan brand — and, in my opinion, before it got really cool."
